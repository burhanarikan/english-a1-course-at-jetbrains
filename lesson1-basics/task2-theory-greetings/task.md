# Greetings and Introductions

## Greetings

| English | When to use |
|---------|-------------|
| **Hello** | Any time — formal or informal |
| **Hi** | Informal — with friends |
| **Good morning** | Before 12:00 (noon) |
| **Good afternoon** | 12:00 – 18:00 |
| **Good evening** | After 18:00 |

## Farewells

| English | When to use |
|---------|-------------|
| **Goodbye / Bye** | Any time |
| **See you later** | When you will meet again soon |
| **Have a nice day** | Polite farewell |
| **Good night** | When someone is going to sleep (22:00+) |

---

## How to Introduce Yourself

Use this simple template:

```
My name is [your name].
I am [your age] years old.
I am from [your country].
```

**Example:**
> My name is Alice.
> I am 20 years old.
> I am from Turkey.
