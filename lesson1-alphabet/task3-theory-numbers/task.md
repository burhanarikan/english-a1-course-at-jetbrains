## 21 – 100 (Pattern Rule)

Combine the tens + ones with a **hyphen (-)**:

| Number | Word |
|--------|------|
| 21 | twenty-**one** |
| 35 | thirty-**five** |
| 47 | forty-**seven** |
| 99 | ninety-**nine** |

### Tens:
20 → twenty | 30 → thirty | 40 → **forty** (no u!) | 50 → fifty | 60 → sixty | 70 → seventy | 80 → eighty | 90 → ninety | 100 → one hundred

---

> ⚠️ **Common mistake:** "Fourty" is WRONG. The correct spelling is **forty** (no letter u).
